About Me

A Passionate Problem Solver

After gaining experience as a Data Analyst in 2022, I decided to formalize my education and formally transition careers.

I wanted to find a position where I could combine most of my previous experience and use it to solve problems that other people may not be able to.

That's what drew my attention to the Marketing subsection of Data Analysis; I have domain experience in Sales and Customer Success which will prove very valuable in giving me unique insights into these problems.

Having experienced every facet of sales in an upfront and managerial/leadership capacity, I now want to turn my attention to working "behind the scenes," but being no less of a team player and leader in doing so!

I'm based in a small city in Atlantic Canada and remote work is what sealed my decision to transition careers.
As such, I can take my eclectic and valuable experience and work anywhere in North America from the comfort of my own home.

My main goals as an analyst are to solve marketing and sales problems you didn't know you had, before you have them.

To that end, I aim to use a combination of different visualization software like PowerBI and Tableau with Python and machine learning in order to be able to tackle any problem.

I am a certified Data Analyst with an intense drive to succeed and I outwork anyone else to achieve my goals.

Join me as I build out this portfolio to its fullest and let me know if I may fit as a solution to your problems!
