Customer Success Reporting Tool

A Tool Aimed at Helping Small Businesses Succeed

In order to help small businesses, who typically struggle with the time it takes to come up with beautiful visualizations to support customer success and data-driven discussions, I have been building a tool which aims to automate as much of the process as possible.

The tool takes a cleaned data sheet and outputs a visualization. My end goal with this tool is to eventually make a web-app, but at this time I am helping clients with this on a consultative basis.

Please reach out if you think we might be a fit to work together or if you find what I'm doing interesting!
